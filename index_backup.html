The Artist

Away, Into the Gilded Dark

RVRND is a Florida-based musical project built at the collision of the sacred, the liminal, and the deeply human — where Southern Gothic myth meets ancient religious tradition, where the mangrove and sand give way to cathedral, and the human soul takes form in the shadow of a blaze.

Dust to dust... but Hell's being alone.

Shaped by the warmth of the sun and the depth of the swamp — RVRND asks the oldest questions about why we are here, what we owe one another, and what shapes us into who we are.

RVRND will brave the darkness and grasp at the light, and you are most welcome here...
